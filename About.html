Sports Bot

Conrad Pereira, Sunny Chaubey, Shania Andrews

Home News Contact/Feedback About

The project will address the following business functions:

  • Entertain ‘customers’ → provide additional information about sports: football, basketball, etc
  • Simplify users’ process of obtaining information about sports (increased organization/flexibility, better information)
  • Conversational abilities with Chatbot
  • Provide sports games reminders and live message updates
  • Allow users to subscribe to a message stream from their favorite sport or a specific game
